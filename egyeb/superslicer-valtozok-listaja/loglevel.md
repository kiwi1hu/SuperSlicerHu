## Logging level

### Leírás

Sets logging sensitivity.

* 0:fatal, 
* 1:error, 
* 2:warning,
* 3:info, 
* 4:debug, 
* 5:trace   

For example. loglevel=2 logs fatal, error and warning level messages.
